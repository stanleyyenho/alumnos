Our Story

Alumnos aims to help high school students make more well-informed decisions for their post secondary-education. The organization was created by university students who saw the opportunity to bridge the gap between alumni who are eager to give back to their high school community, and students seeking to make a comprehensive, well-informed decisison on the trajectory of the next several years of their lives.

Alumnos works to ensure that high school students can have conversations with current post-secondary students who graduated from their high school. Using our extensive Alumni Database system, Alumnos is able to match students to alumni based on universities of interest, programs, and aspirations. This gives them an insider view from someone who was exactly in their position a few short years ago. When the time comes where the students become alumni, our hope is that the impact they felt will encourage them to bring it back full circle and give back to their high school through the place where it began - Alumnos.

How It Works

1. Students with questions about post-secondary education (ex. university experience, transitioning from high school to university, work-life balance, etc.) notify their Alumnos high school ambassador.

2. Their Alumnos high school ambassador uses the Alumnos Database system to connect students with a high school alumna/alumnus that can most accurately answer their questions.

3. Students have a meaningful conversation with an alumna/alumnus from their high school to have their post-secondary questions answered.

Student Benefits

Students gain an insider view of post-secondary education, and have their questions answered from post-secondary students once in their position.

Students receiving admission to the same institution as the alumni they speak to will have a friend to help ease their transition to post-secondary education.

Alumni Benefits

Alumni have the opportunity to stay connected with their high school community and support younger students that were once in their position.

Alumni will be recognized at our annual Alumnos Appreciation Night for their dedication to supporting students.
